Hello World!

My name is Christiana Tinkorang.
I hail from the picturesque Oregon coast, where the rugged beauty of the landscape inspired my love for photography and the great outdoors. Whether capturing the serene sunsets over the Pacific or trekking through lush forests, I find solace and joy in every frame I shoot. Alongside my passion for photography, I have found a love for traveling and seeking new adventures and experiences to broaden my horizons. Amidst my wanderlust, I've ventured out from my birthplace and currently reside in mountainous Utah. I cherish my roles as a devoted wife and a proud mother of three wonderful children, who continuously fill my life with love, laughter, and endless inspiration.

In addition to my love for photography and the outdoors, I've discovered a newfound passion for art in the dynamic realm of website design and management within the tech world. As a proud member of Tech-Moms, I've delved into the intricacies of the tech industry, embracing the challenges and opportunities it presents. Currently serving as a business manager for T-Kay's Real Estate, I've honed my organizational and leadership skills, but my heart remains tethered to the creative realm. With a fervent desire to meld my passion for art and photography with the digital landscape, I aspire to transition into a career focused on web design, management, and advertising, where I can continue to express my creativity while making meaningful contributions to the online sphere.
